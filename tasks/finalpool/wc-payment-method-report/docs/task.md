The finance department would like a breakdown of how customers are paying for their orders in our online store. Please connect to the store and retrieve all orders, then group them by the payment method used to determine which methods are most popular and which generate the most revenue.

Create an Excel file called Payment_Method_Report.xlsx in the workspace with two sheets. The first sheet should be named "Payment Methods" and should have columns Payment_Method, Order_Count, Total_Revenue (rounded to two decimal places), and Avg_Order_Value (total revenue divided by order count, rounded to two decimal places). Sort the rows alphabetically by payment method name.

The second sheet should be named "Summary" with columns Metric and Value. Include the following entries: Total_Orders (the sum of all orders across all payment methods), Total_Revenue (sum of all revenue rounded to two decimal places), Most_Used_Method (the payment method with the highest order count), Highest_Revenue_Method (the payment method generating the most total revenue), and Number_Of_Methods (how many distinct payment methods exist).

After creating the report, send an email from analytics@company.com to finance-lead@company.com with subject "Payment Method Analysis" including the most used payment method and total revenue in the body.
