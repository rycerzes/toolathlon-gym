Food Group Recommendations Per Meal

Each meal should aim to include the following food group servings:

Food GroupServings Per MealExamples
Grains2-3 servingsRice, noodles, bread (1 serving = 1/2 cup cooked)
Protein1-2 servingsMeat, fish, tofu, eggs (1 serving = 85g)
Vegetables2-3 servingsLeafy greens, root vegetables, mushrooms (1 serving = 80g)
Fruits1-2 servingsFresh or dried fruits (1 serving = 80g)

Wellness Scoring Guide

When evaluating recipes for wellness, consider:

  • Higher vegetable content = higher score
  • Lower sugar content = higher score
  • Balanced food groups = higher score
  • Whole ingredients over processed = higher score
  • Appropriate portion sizes = higher score
