Client Wellness Goals

Client: Alex M., 40 years old

Primary Goals:
1. Weight management - maintain healthy weight through balanced nutrition
2. Increase vegetable intake - currently eating only 2 servings/day, target is 5
3. Reduce sugar intake - currently consuming ~50g/day, target is under 25g

Secondary Goals:
- Improve cooking skills with simple, healthy recipes
- Develop a sustainable meal routine
- Learn to estimate nutritional content of home-cooked meals

Constraints:
- Limited cooking time (30 minutes max per meal)
- Moderate budget
- No severe food allergies
- Prefers savory over sweet dishes

Progress Tracking:
- Weekly check-in on vegetable servings
- Monthly weight check
- Daily sugar intake log
