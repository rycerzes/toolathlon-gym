# DEI Board Presentation Outline

## Suggested Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- Title: Diversity & Inclusion Board Report
- Subtitle: Workforce Demographics Analysis
- Date

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
- Total workforce size
- Key demographic highlights
- Overall alignment with industry benchmarks

### Slide 3: Education Distribution
- Internal vs industry benchmark comparison
- Highlight gaps exceeding 3 percentage points
- Department-specific insights

### Slide 4: Age Distribution
- Age group breakdown vs benchmarks
- Identify underrepresented age groups
- Trend implications

### Slide 5: Additional Demographics
- Marital status distribution
- Work-life balance indicators
- Job satisfaction metrics

### Slide 6: Recommendations
- Priority action items
- Measurable targets
- Timeline for next review

## Formatting Notes
- Use clear, data-driven language
- Include both percentages and absolute numbers where relevant
- Highlight areas where gaps exceed benchmark thresholds
