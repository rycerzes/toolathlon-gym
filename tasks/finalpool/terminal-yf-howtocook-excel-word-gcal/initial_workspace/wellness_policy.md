# Corporate Wellness Program Policy - Q2 2026

## Program Goals
The corporate wellness meal program aims to improve employee health and satisfaction
by providing nutritious meal recommendations and educational workshops. The program
runs across all office locations during Q2 2026 (April - June), with planning and
setup occurring in March.

## Target Offices
- New York (headquarters)
- Chicago (regional office)
- Austin (tech hub)

## Nutritional Guidelines
- Meals should cover breakfast, lunch, and dinner options
- Priority given to recipes with lower complexity for easier preparation
- Daily calorie target: 2000-2500 kcal
- Daily protein target: 50-70g
- Emphasis on balanced meals combining vegetables and protein

## Workshop Requirements
- 4 monthly wellness workshops during the planning phase
- Preferred day: Wednesday
- Time slot: lunch hour (12:00 - 13:00)
- Topics should progress from breakfast through dinner planning

## Budget Notes
- Each office has a dedicated monthly wellness budget
- Cost estimates should factor in current commodity price trends
- Food cost calculations should consider energy/transport costs and inflation indicators
