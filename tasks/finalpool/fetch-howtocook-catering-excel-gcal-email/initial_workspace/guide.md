# Wellness Week Menu Planning Guide

## Cost Calculation Formula
- Each ingredient unit costs $0.50 per person
- Estimated cost per recipe = number of unique food ingredients x $0.50 x number of servings (attendees)
- Daily budget limit: $400 per day (total across both meals)
- Weekly budget: $2000 total

## Nutrition Goals
- Average calories per meal should be under 800 kcal
- Include a mix of protein, carbohydrates, and vegetables
- At least 2 vegetarian options across the week

## Menu Structure
- 5 days (Monday through Friday)
- 2 meals per day: Breakfast and Lunch
- All meals serve 50 people

## Vegetarian Classification
- Recipes from the vegetarian (素菜) category are vegetarian
- Breakfast recipes without meat are generally considered vegetarian
- Recipes with meat, poultry, or seafood are not vegetarian
