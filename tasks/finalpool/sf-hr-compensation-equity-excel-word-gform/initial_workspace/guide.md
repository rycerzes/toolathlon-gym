# Pay Equity Audit Methodology

## Experience Band Classification
Employees are classified into experience bands based on their total years of professional experience:
- **Junior**: 0 to 3 years of experience (inclusive)
- **Mid**: 4 to 7 years of experience (inclusive)
- **Senior**: 8 to 12 years of experience (inclusive)
- **Expert**: 13 or more years of experience

## Pay Gap Calculation
For each combination of department and experience band, compare the average salary across all education levels present in that group.

**Pay Gap Percentage** = ((Highest Group Avg Salary - Lowest Group Avg Salary) / Highest Group Avg Salary) x 100

Round to 1 decimal place.

## Equity Ratio
**Equity Ratio** = Minimum Group Average Salary / Maximum Group Average Salary

This is calculated within each department and experience band, comparing across education levels.
Round to 3 decimal places.

## Equity Status Thresholds
- **Concerning**: Equity ratio below 0.85
- **Monitor**: Equity ratio from 0.85 up to (but not including) 0.90
- **Acceptable**: Equity ratio of 0.90 or above

## Overall Equity Score
The average of all equity ratios across all department-experience band combinations, rounded to 2 decimal places.

## Statistical Notes
- Use standard deviation (sample) for salary dispersion
- Median salary is the 50th percentile value
- All monetary values should be rounded to 2 decimal places where specified
