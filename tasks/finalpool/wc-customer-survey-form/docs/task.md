Then create a feedback form titled "Customer Satisfaction Survey" with a description saying "We value your feedback! Please take a moment to share your experience with us." The form should include exactly five questions in this order. The first question should be "How would you rate your overall shopping experience?" and should be a multiple choice question with options Excellent, Good, Average, Poor, and Very Poor. The second question should be "How satisfied are you with the product quality?" as a linear scale from 1 to 5. The third question should be "How likely are you to recommend our store to a friend?" as a linear scale from 1 to 10. The fourth question should be "What did you like most about your purchase?" as a paragraph text question. The fifth question should be "Any suggestions for improvement?" also as a paragraph text question. All questions should be required except the last one about suggestions.

Finally, send an email to each qualifying customer inviting them to fill out the survey. The email subject should be "We'd Love Your Feedback!" and the body should say "Dear [First Name], Thank you for your recent purchase. We would appreciate it if you could take a few minutes to complete our Customer Satisfaction Survey. Your feedback helps us improve our services. Best regards, Customer Experience Team". Replace the first name with each customer's actual first name. Send the emails from survey@store.com.
