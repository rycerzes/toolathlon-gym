# Conference Review Document Template

## Structure

The review document should follow this structure:

### Overview
A brief paragraph describing the review process, the number of papers reviewed, and the general themes covered.

### Per-Paper Review
For each paper, include:
- Paper title as a subsection heading
- A "Strengths" paragraph highlighting what the paper does well
- A "Weaknesses" paragraph noting areas for improvement
- Score breakdown showing each criterion and its score
- Total score

### Comparative Analysis
A discussion comparing the papers' methodological approaches, noting overlaps, complementary techniques, and relative strengths.

### Recommendations
A final list of each paper with its recommendation (Accept/Revise/Reject) and a one-sentence justification.
