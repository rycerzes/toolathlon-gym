# Turnover Risk Assessment Methodology

## Risk Level Classification
High: Department average satisfaction < industry risk threshold
Medium: Department average satisfaction within 0.5 above threshold
Low: Department average satisfaction > threshold + 0.5

## Estimated Turnover Cost
Cost = Employee_Count * Avg_Salary * Industry_Turnover_Rate / 100

## Satisfaction Gap
Gap = Avg_Satisfaction - Risk_Threshold (positive = safer, negative = at risk)
