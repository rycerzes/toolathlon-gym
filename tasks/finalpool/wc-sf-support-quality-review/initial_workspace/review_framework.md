Quarterly Customer Experience Review Framework
================================================

Version 1.2 - Last Updated: January 2026

Review Structure:
This framework guides the quarterly CX quality review process. The review combines data from two primary sources: the e-commerce platform (order data, product issues) and the support center (ticket metrics, satisfaction scores).

Key Performance Indicators:
- Order Issue Rate: Percentage of orders that are refunded or cancelled
- Support Response Time: Average hours from ticket creation to first response
- Customer Satisfaction: Average CSAT score on a 1-5 scale
- Resolution Rate: Percentage of tickets successfully resolved
- NPS: Net Promoter Score (target 50+)

Data Collection Notes:
- E-commerce orders include line items with product information and categories
- An order is considered an "issue order" if its status is "refunded" or "cancelled"
- Support tickets are categorized by priority (High, Medium, Low) and issue type
- All support tickets in the current data set have been resolved

Cross-Reference Methodology:
- Product categories with high issue rates may indicate quality or fulfillment problems
- Support ticket patterns by issue type reveal systemic vs. one-off problems
- Response time compliance by priority level shows operational effectiveness

Presentation Guidelines:
- Board presentations should be 6-8 slides maximum
- Include benchmarks vs. targets on every metric slide
- Use clear category labels for charts and tables
- Highlight areas that exceed or miss targets
