Investor Roadshow Presentation Guide

Slide Structure
Slide 1 Title: Company name, roadshow year, presenter name placeholder. Subtitle should mention the city tour schedule.
Slide 2 Travel Overview: Present a table showing each city, the train number, departure time, arrival time, and seat class. Both Shanghai and Guangzhou visits should be shown.
Slide 3 Financial Highlights: Show revenue and net income for the most recent two available fiscal years. Include year-over-year growth percentage. Show earnings per share.
Slide 4 Stock Performance: Show the 30-day price change percentage for the primary company and the comparison company. Note the current price for each.
Slide 5 Analyst Consensus: Summarize the buy, hold, and sell recommendation counts from analyst data. Note the overall consensus rating.

Excel Sheet Guidelines
Stock_Summary should use the most recent available price from the historical data. Market cap should be expressed in billions USD. Revenue should also be in billions. EPS should be the trailing twelve months figure where available.
Financial_Highlights should use annual figures from the income statement. Use the two most recent fiscal years available. Values should be in billions USD.
Travel_Plan duration should be computed as the number of hours and decimal fraction (e.g., 5.5 for 5 hours 30 minutes).
