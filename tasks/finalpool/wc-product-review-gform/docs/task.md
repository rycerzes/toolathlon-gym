Our e-commerce quality assurance team wants to gather customer feedback on our lowest-rated products. Please look at our product catalog and identify the five products with the lowest average customer ratings, considering only products that have at least some ratings. For products that share the same rating, prefer those with higher total sales numbers first as they represent a broader customer experience.

Create a feedback survey titled "Product Quality Feedback" to collect structured input from customers about these products. The survey should have a description explaining that we are gathering feedback to improve product quality. For each of the five lowest-rated products, add a question asking customers to rate their satisfaction with that specific product on a scale from 1 to 5. Use the product name in each question so respondents know which product is being asked about. Each question should be required.

Next, find which customers have purchased any of these five low-rated products by examining the order history and matching product identifiers in the order line items. Send an email to each of those customers asking them to participate in the product quality feedback survey. Use the subject line "We Value Your Feedback - Product Quality Survey" for each email. The email body should mention the customer's first name and explain that they are receiving this survey because they purchased one of the products under review, and ask them to share their experience.
