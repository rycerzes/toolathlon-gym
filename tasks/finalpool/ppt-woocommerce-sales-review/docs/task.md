The sales team needs a comprehensive monthly sales review package. You are responsible for pulling together order data from our online store, adding market context from a financial data service, and delivering the final materials via email.

Start by retrieving all order information from our online store. Calculate the total number of orders, the number of completed orders, the overall total revenue across all orders, and the average order value. Also determine the top 10 products by revenue, capturing each product name, total units sold, and total revenue generated.

Next, look up the recent performance of the Dow Jones Industrial Average index to provide market context. Get the most recent closing price and compute the approximate percentage change over the past month by comparing the most recent close to the close from roughly one month earlier.

Create an Excel workbook named Monthly_Sales_Review.xlsx in the workspace with three sheets. The first sheet should be named Sales Summary and contain two columns, Metric and Value, with four rows of data: Total_Orders, Completed_Orders, Total_Revenue, and Avg_Order_Value. Round monetary values to two decimal places. The second sheet should be named Top Products and contain three columns: Product_Name, Units_Sold, and Revenue. List the top 10 products sorted by revenue in descending order. The third sheet should be named Market Context and contain three columns: Index, Recent_Close, and Monthly_Change_Pct. Include one row for the Dow Jones index, using the ticker symbol as the Index value. Round the monthly change percentage to two decimal places.

Create a PowerPoint presentation named Sales_Review_Presentation.pptx in the workspace. It should contain at least five slides. The first slide is a title slide with the title Monthly Sales Review. The second slide should present the sales overview, showing total orders, completed orders, total revenue, and average order value. The third slide should highlight the top selling products. The fourth slide should present the market context with the Dow Jones performance data. The fifth slide should contain recommendations or key takeaways for the sales team.

Finally, send an email to sales@company.com with the subject Monthly Sales Review. The email body should summarize the key highlights including the total number of orders, total revenue, the top selling product by revenue, and the Dow Jones monthly performance.
