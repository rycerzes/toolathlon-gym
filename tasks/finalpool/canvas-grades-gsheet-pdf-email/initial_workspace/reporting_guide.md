# Grade Reporting Standards

Standard Grade Scale:
- A: 90-100
- B: 80-89
- C: 70-79
- D: 60-69
- F: Below 60

Pass: Grade C or above (70+)
Academic probation threshold: Below 60

Reports must include:
- Per-course grade distribution
- Pass/fail rates
- Comparison across courses
- Flag courses with pass rate below 70%
