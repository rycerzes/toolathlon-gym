Learning Analytics KPI Definitions

Completion Rate is calculated as the number of graded submissions divided by the total number of submissions, expressed as a percentage. This measures how effectively assignments are being completed and graded.

Average Grade is the mean current score across all students enrolled in a course. This reflects overall student performance and learning outcomes.

Late Submission Rate is the number of late submissions divided by total submissions, expressed as a percentage. High late rates may indicate issues with deadline communication or workload distribution.

Discussion Participation Count is the number of discussion topics created for a course. This serves as a proxy for student engagement and collaborative learning opportunities.

A course meets the overall benchmark when all four KPI targets are satisfied simultaneously. Any single KPI falling below its target disqualifies the course from meeting the overall benchmark.
