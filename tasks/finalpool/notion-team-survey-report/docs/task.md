You are helping the People Operations team at a software company prepare a quarterly review for management. There are two data sources you need to work with.

First, there is a Notion page called "Engineering Team Projects" that contains information about 5 ongoing projects. Each project has a name, a status (Active, Completed, On Hold, or Planning), a lead person, and a deadline. Read this page to understand the current state of the engineering team's work.

Second, there is a Google Form called "Team Satisfaction Survey" that has already collected 8 responses from team members. Each respondent rated four dimensions on a scale of 1 to 5: Leadership, Workload, Communication, and Growth opportunities. Retrieve all the survey responses.

After gathering data from both sources, complete the following:

Create an Excel file called Survey_Analysis.xlsx in your workspace. It should have two sheets. The first sheet should be called "Survey Data" and should contain all 8 individual responses with columns for the respondent name, and their ratings for Leadership, Workload, Communication, and Growth. The second sheet should be called "Summary Statistics" and should contain the average rating for each of the four dimensions across all respondents, rounded to three decimal places.

Create a PowerPoint presentation called Team_Report.pptx in your workspace. The presentation should have at least 5 slides. Include a title slide with "Engineering Team Quarterly Report" as the title. Include a slide summarizing the project statuses from Notion (how many are Active, Completed, On Hold, Planning). Include a slide showing the survey results with the average scores for each dimension. Include a slide with recommendations based on the findings. The presentation should reference both the project data and the survey data.

Send an email to management@company.com with the subject "Engineering Team Quarterly Report" summarizing the key findings. The email should mention the number of projects and their statuses, the survey results including average scores for each dimension, and any areas of concern or recommendations. Make sure to mention the specific average scores in the email body.
