# Corporate Wellness Program Objectives

Goals:
- Provide healthy meal options for employees
- Meet dietary guideline compliance
- Offer variety across the week
- Stay within budget constraints

Nutritional Targets:
- Daily calories: 1800-2200
- Protein: 20-30% of calories
- Carbohydrates: 45-55% of calories
- Fat: 25-35% of calories

Budget: $8-12 per person per meal
