# Investment Roadshow Planning Guide: Beijing to Shanghai

## Overview
This guide defines the output format for planning an investment roadshow trip combining train travel data and stock portfolio analysis.

## Excel Sheet Structure

### Sheet: Travel_Details
Columns:
- Train_Number: Selected train identifier
- Departure_Date: Trip date
- Departure_Time: HH:MM
- Arrival_Time: HH:MM
- Duration_Minutes: Journey duration
- Class: Travel class (first or business)
- Price_CNY: Ticket price per person
- Departure_Station: Full station name
- Arrival_Station: Full station name

### Sheet: Stock_Summary
Columns:
- Ticker: Stock symbol (e.g., AAPL, TSLA)
- Company_Name: Full company name
- Current_Price_USD: Latest closing price
- 52W_High: 52-week high price
- 52W_Low: 52-week low price
- Market_Cap_Billion: Market capitalization in billions USD
- PE_Ratio: Price-to-earnings ratio
- Dividend_Yield_Pct: Annual dividend yield percentage
- YTD_Return_Pct: Year-to-date return percentage

## PowerPoint Slide Requirements
The presentation must contain exactly 4 slides:

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- Title: "Investment Roadshow - [Destination] [Date]"
- Subtitle: "Travel and Portfolio Briefing"
- Include company logo placeholder

### Slide 2: Journey Details
- Selected train number, departure and arrival times
- Journey duration and travel class
- A simple route map visual placeholder

### Slide 3: Portfolio Overview
- Table showing all stocks from the Stock_Summary sheet
- Highlight top performer (highest YTD return) in green
- Highlight any stock with negative YTD return in red

### Slide 4: Meeting Schedule
- List of investor meetings in Shanghai with time slots
- Starting at 14:00, 1.5-hour slots
- Include a note on preparation priorities

## Email Format
- To: roadshow participants
- Subject: "Roadshow Briefing - [Destination] [Date]"
- Body: Summary of selected train, departure time, and top 3 portfolio highlights
- Note filename of attached Excel and PPT files in the email body
