Competitive Landscape Notes - Q1 2026

Our primary competitor TechRival has been aggressive with pricing in recent months.
Their price list is published at http://localhost:30200/index.html and updated regularly.

Key observations from preliminary research:
- TechRival carries about 20 products that overlap with our catalog
- Their Audio and Electronics categories seem most competitive
- We need to identify products where we are significantly overpriced
- The pricing committee wants a full comparison using our regular prices

Focus areas for this analysis:
- Compare our regular prices (not sale prices) against TechRival prices
- Flag products where the price difference exceeds 10% in either direction
- Products within the 10% band should be marked as competitively priced
- Provide a summary with counts and average difference percentages
