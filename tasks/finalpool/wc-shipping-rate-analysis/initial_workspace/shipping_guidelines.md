Internal Shipping Policy Guidelines

Our store uses the following shipping rate structure for customers:
- Free Shipping: Orders qualifying for free shipping pay $0.00
- Standard Shipping: $5.99 flat rate
- Express Shipping: $12.99 flat rate

We currently use a mix of carriers (FedEx, UPS, USPS) for fulfillment.
The actual carrier cost should be compared against what we charge customers.

For this audit, use the 5-10 lbs weight range from the carrier rate tables
as the assumed package weight for all orders. Use USPS standard rate as
the baseline actual cost for Standard Shipping orders, and FedEx express
rate as baseline actual cost for Express Shipping orders.

An order is considered "undercharged" if the actual carrier cost exceeds
what we charged the customer for shipping.

The logistics team at logistics@company.com should be notified about all
undercharged orders, including order IDs and the difference amounts.

Carrier rate tables can be found at http://localhost:30201/index.html -
check both domestic and international pages.
