The CFO has requested a Q4 2024 executive sales briefing covering October through December of that year. You need to pull the relevant quarterly sales data from the company data warehouse, which contains order records along with customer and product information. The analysis should break sales performance down by geographic region and by customer segment.

First, create an Excel workbook called Q4_Executive_Analysis.xlsx in the workspace with two sheets. The first sheet should be named Regional Summary and contain columns Region, Orders, Revenue, and Avg_Order_Value where the average order value is calculated as Revenue divided by Orders and rounded to two decimal places. The second sheet should be named Segment Analysis and contain columns Segment, Orders, Revenue, and Revenue_Share_Pct where the revenue share percentage represents each segment's revenue as a proportion of the total quarterly revenue expressed as a percentage rounded to one decimal place. Sort regions alphabetically in the Regional Summary sheet and sort segments alphabetically in the Segment Analysis sheet.

Next, create a PowerPoint presentation called Q4_Executive_Briefing.pptx in the workspace. It should open with a title slide displaying Q4 2024 Sales Executive Briefing as the title. Following that, include one slide for each region showing that region's name as the slide title along with its order count and revenue figure in the slide body. The region slides should appear in alphabetical order by region name. End the presentation with a summary slide that shows the overall total number of orders and total revenue across all regions for the quarter.

Finally, export the PowerPoint presentation to a PDF file called Q4_Executive_Briefing.pdf saved in the workspace for archival purposes.
