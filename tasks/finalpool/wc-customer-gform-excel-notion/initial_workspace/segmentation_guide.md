# Customer Segmentation Framework

Tier Definitions:
- VIP: Total lifetime spend > $500
- Regular: Total lifetime spend $100-$500
- New: Total lifetime spend < $100

Analysis Dimensions:
- Revenue contribution per segment
- Average order value
- Purchase frequency
- Retention risk assessment

Strategy per Segment:
- VIP: Loyalty rewards, exclusive offers
- Regular: Upsell campaigns, loyalty program enrollment
- New: Welcome series, first-purchase discounts
