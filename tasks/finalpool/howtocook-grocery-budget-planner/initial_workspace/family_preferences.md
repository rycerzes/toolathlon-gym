Family Meal Planning Preferences

Family Size: 4 people (2 adults, 2 children ages 8 and 12)

Dietary Restrictions:
- Shellfish allergy (one child): No shrimp, crab, lobster, clams, or mussels
- No extreme spice for children

Cuisine Preference: Chinese cuisine preferred, occasional Western dishes acceptable

Meal Requirements:
- Breakfast: Simple and quick, ready in under 20 minutes
- Lunch: Moderate portions, can be leftovers from dinner
- Dinner: Main meal of the day, at least one protein and one vegetable

Budget: $150 maximum for the entire week
Target: At least 3 different dishes per day across all meals
Variety: Try not to repeat the same recipe more than twice in a week

Shopping: Prefer to shop at local markets on weekends with a possible midweek trip for fresh items
