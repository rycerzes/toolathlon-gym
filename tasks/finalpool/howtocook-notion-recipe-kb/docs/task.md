Our team wants to build a shared recipe collection as an internal knowledge base. We have access to a recipe database that contains a variety of Chinese home cooking recipes organized by category. Please help us curate a selection of recipes and organize them into a structured format.

First, browse the recipe database and explore what categories and recipes are available. Pick at least 8 recipes from at least 4 different categories such as meat dishes, vegetable dishes, soups, staple foods, breakfast items, or condiments. As you go, record your filtering criteria and the categories you selected in the knowledge graph memory so we can reference your decision process later. For example, note which categories you chose and why.

Next, create an Excel workbook called Recipe_Comparison.xlsx in the workspace. The workbook should have two sheets. The first sheet should be named All Recipes and contain a row for each of the 8 or more recipes you selected, with columns for Recipe Name, Category, Difficulty, Ingredients Count, and Prep Steps Count. Fill in the data based on what you find in the recipe database. The second sheet should be named Top Picks and list your top 5 recommended recipes ranked from best to fifth best, with columns for Rank, Recipe Name, Category, and Reason where you briefly explain why each recipe made your top list.

Finally, create a Notion page titled Team Recipe Collection. Inside that page, create a database with entries for your top 5 recommended recipes. Each entry in the database should have properties for Name, Category, and Difficulty. Make sure the database is well structured so team members can easily browse and filter recipes.
