# Task Guide: Fireship 2025 Monthly Tech Digest

## Overview
Create a monthly digest of top Fireship tech education videos for January through June 2025.

## Deliverables
1. Fireship_2025_Digest.pptx - PowerPoint presentation (7 slides total)
2. 6 calendar events scheduled (first Tuesday of each month, 09:00-09:30)
3. 1 email sent to team@company.com

## Presentation Structure
- Slide 1 (Title): "Fireship 2025 Monthly Tech Digest"
- Slides 2-7: One slide per month (January 2025 through June 2025)
  - Slide title: Month name and year (e.g. "January 2025")
  - Content: Top video title, view count, duration in minutes (1 decimal), total videos that month

## Calendar Event Pattern
- Title: "Monthly Tech Digest Review - [Month Year]"
- Dates: 2025-01-07, 2025-02-04, 2025-03-04, 2025-04-01, 2025-05-06, 2025-06-03
- Time: 09:00 to 09:30
- Description: Must mention the top video title for that month

## Email
- To: team@company.com
- Subject: "Fireship 2025 Monthly Digest Ready"
- Body: List all 6 monthly top videos with titles and view counts
