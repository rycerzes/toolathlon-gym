# Corporate Wellness Nutrition Program Requirements

## Program Overview
Design and implement an evidence-based workplace nutrition program for employees at MegaCorp Industries. The program should combine practical, easy-to-prepare recipes with insights from published nutritional science research.

## Target Population
- Total eligible employees: 200
- Located across 3 office buildings on the same campus
- Mix of desk-based and light-activity roles
- Age range: 24-62, median age 38

## Goals
1. Improve overall employee dietary habits through accessible, research-backed meal recommendations
2. Accommodate common dietary restrictions prevalent among staff
3. Provide weekly menu plans that meet established nutritional targets
4. Gather employee feedback to iteratively improve the program
5. Stay within the allocated budget

## Budget
- Total annual budget: $50,000
- Must cover: ingredients guidance, cooking workshops, nutritional consultations, survey tools, and administration
- Target cost per employee per week for recipe ingredients: $35

## Constraints
- Recipes must be feasible for home preparation (difficulty level 4 or below preferred)
- All menus must provide options for the top 3 most common dietary restrictions among employees
- Program must be supported by at least 3 peer-reviewed research papers on workplace nutrition
- Weekly menus should cover Monday through Friday (breakfast, lunch, dinner)

## Success Metrics
- Employee participation rate > 40% within first quarter
- Dietary compliance (meeting nutritional targets) > 75% for planned menus
- Employee satisfaction score > 3.5/5 on post-program survey
