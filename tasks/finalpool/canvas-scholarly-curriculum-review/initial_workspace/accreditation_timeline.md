Required Deliverables for Self-Study:
1. Course compliance matrix showing all courses meet minimum standards
2. Faculty adequacy report demonstrating sufficient instructor coverage
3. Assessment diversity evidence (quizzes, assignments, discussions)
4. Scholarly literature review supporting pedagogical approaches
5. Student outcome data and enrollment trends

Assessment Types:
The accreditation body recognizes three primary assessment types:
- Quizzes (formative assessment)
- Assignments (summative assessment)
- Discussions (participatory assessment)

Each course should ideally incorporate at least two of these three types.
