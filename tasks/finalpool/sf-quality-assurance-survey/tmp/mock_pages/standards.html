Target Metrics Best Practices

Best Practices Checklist

Ticket Handling Standards

  • Acknowledge all High priority tickets within 30 minutes of creation
  • Provide status updates every 2 hours for High priority tickets
  • Document root cause analysis for all Bug and Incident type tickets
  • Follow up with customer within 24 hours after resolution
  • Escalate tickets exceeding SLA thresholds immediately to team lead

Communication Standards

  • Use professional and empathetic language in all customer interactions
  • Include steps to reproduce for technical issues
  • Provide estimated resolution timeline in first response
  • Offer alternative solutions when primary fix is delayed

Quality Scoring Methodology

Each agent is scored on three dimensions:

Response Time Score: Percentage of tickets where first response was within the priority-specific target time.

Satisfaction Score: Average customer satisfaction rating across all resolved tickets.

Resolution Score: Percentage of assigned tickets successfully resolved.

An agent is flagged for improvement if any single dimension falls below its target threshold.
