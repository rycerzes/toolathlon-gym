# Quality Assurance Checklist

## Scoring Methodology

Each support agent is evaluated on three primary dimensions:

1. Response Time: Compare average first response time against the published target for each priority level.
2. Customer Satisfaction: Average CSAT score across all handled tickets, measured on a 1-5 scale.
3. Resolution Rate: Percentage of tickets the agent successfully resolved out of total assigned.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Agents meeting all three targets receive a "Meets Standards" rating.
- Agents failing one dimension receive a "Needs Improvement" rating with specific action items.
- Agents failing two or more dimensions are flagged for "Coaching Required" status.

## QA Review Frequency

Monthly reviews are conducted by the QA team. Quarterly summaries are provided to department leadership.

## Data Sources

- Ticket data from our support center database
- Quality targets from the internal standards portal
- Agent roster from team management system
