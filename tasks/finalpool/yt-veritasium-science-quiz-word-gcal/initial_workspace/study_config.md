Study Configuration Guide

Difficulty Rating Scale
Rate each video from 1 to 5 based on the scientific depth of content.
1 means introductory and suitable for high school students.
5 means graduate-level requiring prior advanced knowledge.

Topics to Cover
Focus on videos covering the following scientific domains: physics, mathematics, engineering, biology, psychology, and chemistry. Use the primary domain as the Topic label in the spreadsheet.

Question Format
Write three questions per video. Questions should test understanding of the core concept explained in the video. Avoid trivial recall questions. Aim for conceptual understanding and application.

Conflict Handling
If a scheduled study session date overlaps with an existing calendar event, note this at the end of the Word document under a section titled "Scheduling Notes".
