# Task Guide: Fireship Engagement Analysis

## Overview
Analyze engagement rates for all Fireship YouTube videos and create a research report.

## Definition
Engagement Rate (%) = (like_count / view_count) * 100, rounded to 3 decimal places

## Deliverables
1. Cloud spreadsheet titled "Fireship Engagement Analysis" with two sheets
2. Engagement_Analysis_Report.docx Word document
3. Email to research@company.com

## Sheet 1: Engagement_Data
Columns: Video_ID, Title, Published_Month (YYYY-MM), Duration_Sec (integer), View_Count, Like_Count, Engagement_Rate_Pct
Sort: Descending by Engagement_Rate_Pct

## Sheet 2: Monthly_Summary
Columns: Month (YYYY-MM), Video_Count, Avg_Engagement_Rate, Top_Video_Title
Sort: Ascending by Month

## Word Document Sections (exact headings required)
1. Overview
2. Methodology
3. Key Findings (include top 3 most engaging videos with rates)
4. Monthly Trends
5. Conclusions

## Email
- To: research@company.com
- Subject: "Fireship YouTube Engagement Analysis"
- Body: total video count + at least one top engaging video name
