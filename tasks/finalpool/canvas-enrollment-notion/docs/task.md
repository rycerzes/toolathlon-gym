The university registrar's office needs a comprehensive enrollment overview across all courses in the learning management system. Please retrieve the list of all courses and then gather enrollment data for each course, counting how many students, teachers, and teaching assistants are enrolled in each one. The enrollment type can be determined from the enrollment role where student enrollments, teacher enrollments, and TA enrollments should each be counted separately.

After collecting all the enrollment data, create a database in the project management workspace titled "Course Enrollment Tracker" to serve as a centralized tracking board. Each course should have its own entry in this database. The properties for each entry should include the course name as the title, and additional properties for Student_Count, Teacher_Count, TA_Count, and Total_Enrollment where the total is the sum of students, teachers, and TAs.

Finally, send an email to admin@university.example.com with the subject "Course Enrollment Summary Report". The body of the email should include the total number of courses, the overall total enrollment across all courses, and a brief listing of the top five courses by total enrollment showing each course name and its total enrollment count.
