Health Thresholds Scoring Formula

Customer Health Scoring Portal

This portal defines the health scoring methodology used to classify customer accounts and prioritize engagement.

Health Status Thresholds

StatusScore RangeAction Required
Healthy Score >= 80 Regular check-ins, upsell opportunities
At Risk Score 50 to 79 Proactive outreach, satisfaction survey
Critical Score < 50 Immediate follow-up call, executive escalation

Follow-Up Policy

For accounts classified as Critical, the customer success team must schedule a follow-up call within 5 business days. Critical accounts with health scores below 15 require executive-level intervention and should be scheduled as 30-minute calls starting from the Monday after the analysis date, with one call per day at 10:00 AM.

Quick Reference:
Healthy: score >= 80 | At Risk: 50-79 | Critical: < 50
