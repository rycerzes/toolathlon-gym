Health Thresholds Scoring Formula

Health Score Calculation

health_score = 0.4 * recency_score + 0.3 * frequency_score + 0.3 * monetary_score

Score Components

Recency Score (weight: 0.4)

recency_score = max(0, 100 - days_since_last_order)

Measures how recently the customer placed their last order. A customer who ordered today gets 100 points. The score decreases by 1 point for each day since their last order, with a minimum of 0.

Note: Use the current date at the time of analysis to compute days since last order.

Frequency Score (weight: 0.3)

frequency_score = min(100, order_count * 10)

Measures order frequency. Each order contributes 10 points, capped at 100 (10+ orders).

Monetary Score (weight: 0.3)

monetary_score = min(100, lifetime_value / 50)

Measures customer spending. The lifetime value from the customer record divided by 50, capped at 100 (lifetime value of $5,000+).

Implementation Notes

All three component scores range from 0 to 100. The weighted sum produces a health score also ranging from 0 to 100. Round the final health score to 2 decimal places.
