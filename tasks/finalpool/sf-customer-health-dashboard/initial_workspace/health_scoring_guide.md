# Customer Health Scoring Guide

## Overview

Customer health scoring helps our customer success team prioritize their efforts by identifying accounts that need immediate attention versus those that are thriving.

## Score Categories

Accounts are classified into three tiers based on their computed health score. The specific thresholds and calculation formula are maintained on our internal scoring portal.

## Escalation Procedures

- Critical accounts should receive immediate follow-up calls
- At-risk accounts should be monitored monthly
- Healthy accounts are candidates for upselling and expansion

## Data Sources

- Customer master data from our sales database (customer details, segment, region)
- Order history from the orders table (dates, amounts, frequency)
- Health score formula from the internal portal

## Reporting

The customer success team maintains a dashboard in Notion for tracking critical accounts and a calendar of follow-up activities.
