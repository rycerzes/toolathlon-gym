Margin Targets by Product Category

The company targets the following profit margins by category:

Watches: 45% target margin (high-value items with significant gold content)
Electronics: 25% target margin (competitive pricing environment)
Audio: 30% target margin (brand differentiation allows premium pricing)
Cameras: 28% target margin (niche market with steady demand)
TV and Home Theater: 20% target margin (high competition, volume-driven)
Home Appliances: 22% target margin (commodity products with thin margins)

Categories with commodity sensitivity above 20% should be reviewed for pricing
adjustments whenever gold prices move more than 5% in a 30-day period.

Recommended actions:
- Review Pricing: For categories with sensitivity >= 20%
- Monitor: For categories with sensitivity < 20%
