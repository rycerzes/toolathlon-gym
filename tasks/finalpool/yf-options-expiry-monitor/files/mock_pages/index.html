Options Position Monitoring Portal

Risk management parameters for options position monitoring. Last updated: 2026-03-05.

Risk Classification Thresholds

High Implied Volatility Threshold: 40%

Positions with average implied volatility above 40% are classified as "High IV" and require review.

Near Expiry Alert Window: 7 days

Positions expiring within 7 days of the current date (on or before 2026-03-14) need immediate attention.

Deep ITM Threshold: 0.80 (strike / current price)

A call option with strike below 80% of the current stock price is "Deep In-The-Money."

Deep OTM Threshold: 1.20 (strike / current price)

A call option with strike above 120% of the current stock price is "Deep Out-of-The-Money."

Position Guidelines

For "High IV + Near Expiry" positions: Consider closing or rolling immediately.
For "Near Expiry" positions: Schedule review before expiration.
For "High IV" positions: Monitor daily for changes in implied volatility.
Create calendar alerts for all near-expiry positions with event title "Options Expiry Alert: [SYMBOL]" on the expiration date from 09:00 to 09:30.
