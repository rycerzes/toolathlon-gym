# Options Strategy Guidelines

## Current Strategy

The firm employs a combination of covered calls and protective puts on its equity portfolio. The primary objective is income generation through premium collection while maintaining downside protection.

## Position Sizing Rules

Each equity position may have covered calls sold against up to 50% of the shares held. Protective puts may be purchased for up to 25% of the position value.

## Monitoring Requirements

All options positions must be monitored daily. Positions approaching expiration require special attention to avoid unwanted assignment or expiration of valuable options.

## Risk Thresholds

Options with implied volatility significantly above historical norms should be flagged for review. The firm uses a 40% implied volatility threshold as the alert level.
