# Student Electronics Purchase Behavior Analysis Guide

## Methodology

This analysis investigates the relationship between academic performance in Applied Analytics courses and electronics purchasing behavior at the university bookstore.

### Data Sources
- Student enrollment and submission records from courses 1 and 2 (Applied Analytics sections)
- Bookstore order and product data focusing on Electronics and Cameras categories
- Student-to-customer mapping via the bookstore loyalty program registry

### Analysis Steps
1. Calculate per-student average submission scores for Applied Analytics courses
2. Aggregate electronics and camera purchases per matched customer
3. Cross-reference using the loyalty registry to link academic and purchase records
4. Compute Pearson correlation between average scores and total electronics spending
5. Generate actionable recommendations based on the correlation direction

### Output Requirements
- Excel workbook with four analysis sheets
- PowerPoint presentation with five slides
- Email reports to bookstore management and academic affairs
