The strategy team is conducting a competitive positioning assessment ahead of the upcoming board meeting. A mock industry intelligence portal is available at http://localhost:30216 and contains an HTML page with detailed competitor profiles. The page displays a table listing five competitors, each with their company name, estimated annual revenue in millions, market share percentage, number of products offered, and average customer satisfaction score on a 1 to 5 scale.

Start by navigating to the competitor intelligence portal and extracting all five competitor profiles from the HTML table. Then query the company data warehouse to pull internal sales performance metrics, specifically total revenue by region and total revenue by customer segment. The warehouse contains approximately 20,000 orders across five regions (Europe, Asia Pacific, North America, Middle East, Latin America) and four customer segments (Consumer, Enterprise, Government, SMB).

Once both datasets are collected, create a Notion database titled "Competitive Intelligence Tracker" that stores each competitor as a page entry. Each page should include properties for Company Name, Revenue (number), Market Share (number), Product Count (number), and Satisfaction Score (number). Also add a property called "Competitive Position" as a text field that categorizes each competitor as "Leader" if their market share is 20% or above, "Challenger" if between 10% and 19.9%, or "Niche" if below 10%.

Next, create an Excel workbook called "Competitive_Analysis.xlsx" in the workspace with three sheets. The first sheet should be called "Competitor Profiles" and contain the five competitors with columns for Company, Revenue_M, Market_Share_Pct, Product_Count, Satisfaction, and Position. The second sheet should be called "Internal Performance" and have two sections: one showing revenue by region with columns Region, Orders, and Revenue, and another showing revenue by segment with columns Segment, Orders, and Revenue. The third sheet should be called "SWOT Summary" and contain a strategic comparison with columns for Factor, Internal_Value, Competitor_Avg, Gap, and Assessment. Include at least four factors: Total Revenue comparing total internal revenue against average competitor revenue, Market Breadth comparing internal region count against average competitor product count, Customer Base comparing internal total orders against competitor average, and Satisfaction comparing an estimated internal score of 4.2 against average competitor satisfaction.

Finally, send an email from strategy@company.com to board@company.com with the subject "Competitive Positioning Analysis - Q1 2026". The email body should summarize the competitive landscape, mention the leading competitor by name and market share, and note the company's total internal revenue and strongest region.

Save all output files to the workspace directory.
