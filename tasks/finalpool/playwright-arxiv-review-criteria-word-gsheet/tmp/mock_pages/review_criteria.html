ML Conference 2026 - Peer Review Scoring Rubric

Official guidelines for all reviewers. Please evaluate each paper on the following criteria.

1. Technical Soundness (Score 1-5)

ScoreDescription
5 - ExcellentThe methodology is rigorous and well-justified. All claims are supported by solid theoretical or empirical evidence. Experimental design is thorough with appropriate baselines and ablation studies.
4 - GoodThe methodology is sound with minor issues. Most claims are well-supported. Some additional experiments or analysis would strengthen the paper.
3 - AdequateThe core methodology is acceptable but has notable gaps. Some claims lack sufficient support. Key comparisons may be missing.
2 - WeakSignificant methodological concerns. Several claims are not well-supported. Important baselines or controls are absent.
1 - PoorFundamental flaws in methodology or reasoning. Claims are unsubstantiated.

2. Novelty (Score 1-5)

ScoreDescription
5 - Highly NovelIntroduces a fundamentally new concept, method, or perspective that could significantly impact the field. First-of-its-kind contribution.
4 - NovelPresents meaningful new ideas or combines existing ideas in a creative way. Advances the state of the art in a notable manner.
3 - Somewhat NovelIncremental improvement over existing work. Some new elements but primarily builds on well-established ideas.
2 - Limited NoveltyMostly replicates existing approaches with minor variations. Limited new contributions.
1 - Not NovelNo meaningful new contribution. Straightforward application of known methods.

3. Clarity (Score 1-5)

ScoreDescription
5 - ExcellentExceptionally well-written. Clear structure, precise language, well-designed figures and tables. Easy to follow for the target audience.
4 - GoodWell-written overall with minor areas that could be clearer. Good organization and presentation.
3 - AdequateUnderstandable but could benefit from significant revision. Some sections are unclear or poorly organized.
2 - Below AverageDifficult to follow in several places. Poor organization or unclear explanations of key concepts.
1 - PoorVery difficult to understand. Major rewriting needed.

4. Overall Recommendation

RecommendationAverage Score RangeDescription
Accept4.0 and aboveStrong contribution to the field. Ready for publication with minor revisions at most.
Weak Accept3.5 - 3.9Positive contribution but with some concerns. Would benefit from revisions.
Borderline3.0 - 3.4Mixed quality. Significant strengths but also notable weaknesses.
Weak Reject2.5 - 2.9Below the acceptance threshold. Substantial revisions needed.
RejectBelow 2.5Does not meet the standards for publication at this venue.

Review Structure Requirements

Each review must include the following sections:

  • Summary: 2-3 sentences describing the paper's main contribution
  • Technical Soundness: Score and justification referencing specific methods or experiments
  • Novelty: Score and justification comparing to prior work
  • Clarity: Score and justification about writing quality and organization
  • Overall Recommendation: Accept/Weak Accept/Borderline/Weak Reject/Reject with rationale
