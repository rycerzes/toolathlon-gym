Radio DJ Show Production Guide

This guide explains how to produce a radio show script from an Afrobeat mix video.

Creating Track Introductions
For each track in the playlist, write a one-to-two sentence introduction that a DJ would speak before the song plays. The introduction should mention the artist name and one interesting fact or brief description about the song. Keep the tone energetic and engaging. For example: This next track comes from the legendary Burna Boy, whose smooth Afropop fusion style has taken the world by storm.

Show Structure
The show runs for two hours total. The opening monologue lasts about five minutes and introduces the genre and the energy of the session. The playlist section covers approximately fifty to sixty minutes of music with brief DJ commentary between tracks. The closing segment lasts about five minutes and wraps up the show with a sign-off.

Timed Transcript Analysis
The timed transcript provides each segment of text with its start time in seconds and duration. Use the start_time field to estimate where each song begins. Convert seconds to minutes by dividing by 60 for the Start_Min column in the Playlist sheet.

Show Scheduling
The Afrobeat Sunday Show airs every Sunday in April 2026 from 20:00 to 22:00. There are four Sundays in April 2026: April 5, April 12, April 19, and April 26.
