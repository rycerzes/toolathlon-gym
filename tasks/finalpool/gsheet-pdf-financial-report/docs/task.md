The finance team needs a comprehensive annual financial report for fiscal year 2024 covering January through December. The company data warehouse contains all order, customer, and product records needed for this analysis.

Start by pulling the full year 2024 sales data from the data warehouse. You will need order-level data including amounts and dates, as well as the product catalog for product-level analysis.

Create an Excel workbook named FY2024_Financial_Analysis.xlsx in the workspace with two sheets. The first sheet should be called "Quarterly Revenue" and must contain the columns Quarter, Revenue, Order_Count, and Avg_Order_Value. The Quarter column should use the labels Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q4 corresponding to the standard calendar quarters of 2024. Revenue is the sum of all order amounts in that quarter. Order_Count is the number of orders in that quarter. Avg_Order_Value is calculated as Revenue divided by Order_Count, rounded to two decimal places. Sort the rows by quarter in ascending order from Q1 to Q4.

The second sheet should be called "Top Products" and must list the ten products with the highest total revenue across all of fiscal year 2024. Include the columns Product_Name, Category, Units_Sold, and Revenue. Units_Sold is the total quantity of that product ordered during the year. Revenue is the total order amount for that product. Sort by Revenue in descending order.

After the Excel file is ready, sync the quarterly revenue data to a shared dashboard. Create a new spreadsheet titled "FY2024 Dashboard" with a sheet called "Quarterly Revenue" containing the same columns and data as the first sheet of the Excel file.

Next, create a Word document named FY2024_Financial_Report.docx in the workspace. The document should have the title "FY2024 Annual Financial Report" as a heading at the top. The document must include at least four sections with their own headings: Executive Summary, Quarterly Performance, Product Analysis, and Outlook. Each section should contain narrative text that references actual revenue figures, order counts, and product names from the data you analyzed. The Executive Summary should mention the total annual revenue. The Quarterly Performance section should discuss each quarter's results. The Product Analysis section should highlight the top performing products. The Outlook section should provide a forward-looking perspective.

Finally, export the Word document to a PDF file named FY2024_Financial_Report.pdf saved in the workspace. This PDF serves as the formal distribution copy of the annual report.
