Employee Dietary Restrictions Survey Results

Based on our Q4 2025 employee survey (847 respondents), the following dietary restrictions were reported:

  • Vegetarian: 23% of respondents - Must offer at least one fully vegetarian main dish option daily
  • Low-Sodium: 18% of respondents - Aim for dishes with reduced soy sauce and salt
  • Gluten-Free: 12% of respondents - Ensure at least one gluten-free option available
  • No Pork: 15% of respondents - Label pork-containing dishes clearly
Note: The wellness program runs from March 10, 2026 through March 14, 2026 (Monday through Friday). Menu planning should focus on balanced Chinese cuisine that meets our diverse workforce needs. All nutritional estimates should be calculated per single serving.
