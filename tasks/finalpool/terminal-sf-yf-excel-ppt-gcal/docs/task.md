You are preparing a quarterly investment committee briefing for the CFO. The goal is to combine internal sales performance data with external market context and schedule the briefing around existing calendar commitments.

Start by reading briefing_template.json in your workspace. It defines the expected report structure and KPIs the committee cares about. Also read committee_members.csv, which lists the attendees and their roles. Finally, open previous_quarter.xlsx, which contains last quarter's summary data you will use for comparison.

From the enterprise data warehouse, query the sales database to get quarterly revenue broken down by product brand. Specifically, pull Q3 2025 (July through September) and Q4 2025 (October through December) revenue for each brand. Also query the top 10 customers by total spend during the full year 2025, including their name, segment, total spend amount, and order count.

Write a Python script called compute_growth.py in your workspace that takes the quarterly revenue data and computes the quarter-over-quarter growth rate for each brand as a percentage, calculated as (Q4 revenue minus Q3 revenue) divided by Q3 revenue times 100. The script should also rank brands by Q4 revenue from highest to lowest. Run the script using the terminal.

Next, look up recent stock performance for GOOGL, AMZN, JPM, and XOM from the financial data service. Also retrieve the gold price trend using the symbol GC=F. For each symbol, note the most recent closing price and calculate the percentage change from the price five trading days earlier.

Write a second Python script called market_comparison.py in your workspace. This script should compare the company's overall Q4 revenue growth rate against the stock market performance of the symbols above, outputting a file called market_comparison.json that contains each symbol, its recent price, its five-day percentage change, and the company's QoQ revenue growth for context. Run this script via the terminal.

Check the shared calendar for the week of March 9 through March 13, 2026 to see existing commitments. Find the first available two-hour slot between 9 AM and 5 PM on any of those days. If no two-hour block is free, pick the earliest morning slot starting at 8 AM. Create a calendar event called "Q4 Investment Committee Briefing" in that available slot. The description should mention that this is the quarterly briefing covering sales performance and market context, and list the committee members from the CSV file.

Create an Excel workbook called Investment_Committee_Briefing.xlsx in your workspace with four sheets. The first sheet named Revenue_By_Brand should have columns for Brand, Q3_Revenue, Q4_Revenue, QoQ_Growth_Pct, and Rank. Include one row per brand, sorted by Q4 revenue descending. The second sheet named Top_Customers should have columns for Customer_Name, Segment, Total_Spend, and Order_Count. Include the top 10 customers sorted by total spend descending. The third sheet named Market_Context should have columns for Symbol, Recent_Price, Five_Day_Change_Pct, and Trend where Trend is "Up" if the change is positive, "Down" if negative, or "Flat" if within plus or minus 0.5 percent. Include rows for GOOGL, AMZN, JPM, XOM, and GC=F. The fourth sheet named Executive_Summary should have columns for Metric and Value. Include rows for Total Q4 Revenue, Total Q3 Revenue, Overall QoQ Growth Pct, Top Brand by Q4 Revenue, Number of Brands with Positive Growth, and Briefing Date.

Create a PowerPoint presentation called Committee_Briefing.pptx in your workspace with six slides. The first slide should be a title slide with "Q4 2025 Investment Committee Briefing" and today's date. The second slide should present the revenue overview showing top brands by Q4 revenue and their growth rates. The third slide should cover customer analysis highlighting the top customers and their segment distribution. The fourth slide should present market context with the stock performance data and gold price trend. The fifth slide should provide a combined outlook discussing how internal sales growth compares to market performance. The sixth slide should list next steps and action items including the scheduled briefing date.

Finally, write a plain text file called briefing_notes.txt in your workspace summarizing the key findings: overall revenue trend, standout brands, notable customer concentration, and market conditions.
