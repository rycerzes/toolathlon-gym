Key Assumptions for Regional Sales Forecast
=============================================

1. Economic conditions will remain consistent with current indicators through Q1 2026.

2. No major market disruptions, trade policy changes, or geopolitical events will significantly impact sales.

3. Product portfolio and pricing will remain stable through the forecast period.

4. Sales team capacity and coverage will remain at current levels.

5. Currency exchange rates will not materially affect regional revenues.

6. The 3-month trailing average (Oct-Dec 2025) provides a reasonable baseline for near-term projections.

7. GDP growth rates serve as a reasonable proxy for regional demand changes.

8. The forecast should be used as directional guidance and updated monthly with actual results.

9. Consumer confidence and inflation indicators provide additional context but are not directly used in the forecast formula.

10. All revenue figures are in USD equivalent.
