Literature Review Criteria

Focus Areas:
- Transformer architectures and self-attention mechanisms
- Novel training approaches and optimization methods
- Applications of transformers to different domains (NLP, vision, multimodal)
- Scaling properties and efficiency improvements

For each paper, document:
- The core methodology and architectural innovations
- How it relates to or improves upon prior work
- Key experimental findings and benchmarks
- Potential relevance to the proposed research

Citation analysis should identify how papers in the collection reference each other, particularly methodological dependencies and evolutionary relationships.
