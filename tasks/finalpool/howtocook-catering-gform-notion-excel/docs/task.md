You are a catering planner for a company team lunch event. The event is for 20 people with a budget of $25 per person. You need to plan a diverse menu, collect dietary preferences from attendees, document the menu, and track the budget.

Your task has four parts.

Part 1: Search the recipe database for 6 to 8 recipes suitable for a team lunch catering event. Select a mix of main dishes and side dishes. For each recipe, note the recipe name, category (such as meat, vegetable, staple, or similar), and estimated number of servings.

Part 2: Create a Google Forms survey titled "Team Lunch Dietary Preferences" to collect dietary information from the 20 attendees. The survey must contain at least 3 questions covering the following topics: dietary restrictions or preferences (such as vegetarian, vegan, nut allergy, gluten-free, or none), any specific food allergies to be aware of, and any additional comments or requests. Use appropriate question types for each question.

Part 3: Create a Notion page titled "Team Lunch Menu" documenting the catering plan. The page must include a description of the event (20 people, $25 per person budget), a list of all selected recipes with their categories and serving information, and any notes about accommodating dietary restrictions.

Part 4: Create an Excel file called Catering_Budget.xlsx in the workspace with two sheets. The first sheet must be named "Menu" with the following columns in this exact order: Recipe_Name, Category, Servings, Estimated_Cost_Per_Person. Add one row for each of your selected recipes with a reasonable estimated cost per person. The second sheet must be named "Summary" with two columns: Metric and Value. Include the following rows: Total_Dishes (number of recipes selected), Total_Budget (total budget for 20 people, which is 500), Budget_Per_Person (which is 25).

The initial workspace contains Event_Brief.md describing the event details and requirements.
