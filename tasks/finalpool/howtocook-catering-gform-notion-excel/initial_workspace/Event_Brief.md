Event Brief: Company Team Lunch

Event Details:
- Event: Company Team Lunch
- Number of Attendees: 20 people
- Budget: $25 per person (Total: $500)
- Format: Catered team lunch, served buffet style
- Occasion: Q1 team celebration and planning session

Catering Requirements:
The menu should include a variety of dishes to accommodate different tastes and dietary needs.
We need both main dishes and side dishes for a complete meal.
Estimated servings per dish should be sufficient for 20 people.

Dietary Accommodation:
We have team members with various dietary needs. Please ensure we collect dietary preferences
from all attendees before finalizing the menu. Common restrictions in our team may include
vegetarian, vegan, nut allergies, and gluten-free requirements.

Budget Guidelines:
- Total Budget: $500 (20 people x $25)
- Aim for 6-8 dishes total
- Mix of main dishes and sides
- Beverages are separate and not included in this budget

Timeline:
- Dietary preference survey to be sent at least 5 days before the event
- Final menu to be confirmed 3 days before the event
- Catering setup 1 hour before event start
