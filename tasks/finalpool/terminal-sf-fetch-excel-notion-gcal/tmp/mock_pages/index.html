Methodology

Benchmarks are derived from a survey of 500+ technology and service companies, weighted by company size. Response time is measured from ticket creation to first agent response. Customer satisfaction is measured on a 1-5 Likert scale collected post-resolution. SLA compliance is defined as the percentage of tickets resolved within the priority-defined time window.
