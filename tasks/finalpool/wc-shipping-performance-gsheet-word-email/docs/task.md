You are a logistics analyst responsible for monthly shipping performance reporting. Analyze WooCommerce shipping data and produce a performance dashboard and report.

First, retrieve orders from the WooCommerce store (include completed, processing, on-hold, and pending orders). Analyze each order's shipping method. Group orders by shipping method (Standard Shipping, Express Shipping, and orders with no shipping charge), and for each group calculate the total number of orders, the total revenue, and the average order value.

Next, create a Google Sheet titled "Shipping Performance Dashboard" with the following columns: Zone_Name, Order_Count, Total_Revenue, Avg_Order_Value. Populate it with one row per shipping method group. Use "Standard Shipping" for flat-rate standard shipping orders, "Express Shipping" for express shipping orders, and "Free Shipping" for orders with no shipping line. Round monetary values to 2 decimal places.

Then, create a Word document called Shipping_Performance_Report.docx in the workspace. The document must include: a heading "Shipping Performance Report", an executive summary paragraph explaining the overall shipping performance, a table showing the same shipping zone data (Zone_Name, Order_Count, Total_Revenue, Avg_Order_Value), and at least one more paragraph with analysis or recommendations. The document must have at least 3 paragraphs total.

Finally, send an email to logistics@store.example.com from operations@store.example.com with the subject "Monthly Shipping Performance Report". The email body should summarize the key shipping metrics.

Refer to the Shipping_Strategy.pdf file in your workspace for context on shipping policies.
