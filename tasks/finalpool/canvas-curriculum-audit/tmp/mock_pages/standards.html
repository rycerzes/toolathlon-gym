Content Quality Standards

These metrics ensure appropriate difficulty and engagement levels across all courses.

Assessment Difficulty Targets

Metric Target Range Interpretation
Average Assignment Score 60 - 85 Scores in this range indicate appropriate difficulty. Below 60 suggests content is too challenging; above 85 suggests insufficient rigor.
Quiz Pass Rate 70% minimum At least 70% of students should pass each quiz to confirm learning objectives are achievable.
Module Completion 80% minimum At least 80% of enrolled students should complete each module.

Course Compliance Scoring

Each course is scored on a pass/fail basis for each structural requirement. A course is considered compliant only if ALL requirements are met. Non-compliant courses must submit a remediation plan within 30 days of the audit.
