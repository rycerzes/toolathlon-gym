These standards must be met by all courses for accreditation compliance.

Course Structure Requirements

Requirement Minimum Threshold Description
Assignments 8 Each course must have at least 8 graded assignments
Quizzes 3 Each course must include at least 3 quizzes for formative assessment
Modules 4 Course content must be organized into at least 4 modules
Syllabus Required An active syllabus must be published and accessible to students
