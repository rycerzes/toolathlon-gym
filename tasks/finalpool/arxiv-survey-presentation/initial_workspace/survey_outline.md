Proposed Survey Structure: Recent Advances in NLP Reasoning

Research Questions:
How have large language models improved their reasoning capabilities in the past two years?
What are the main methodological approaches: prompting, training, or architecture?
Which evaluation benchmarks are most commonly used?

Planned Sections:
1. Introduction and motivation
2. Background on language models
3. Prompting-based methods (chain-of-thought, tree-of-thought, etc.)
4. Training-based methods (RLHF, instruction tuning, etc.)
5. Evaluation and benchmarks
6. Discussion and future directions

Key comparison dimensions:
- Methodology type (prompting vs. training vs. architecture)
- Scale of models used
- Evaluation benchmarks and metrics
- Improvement over baselines
