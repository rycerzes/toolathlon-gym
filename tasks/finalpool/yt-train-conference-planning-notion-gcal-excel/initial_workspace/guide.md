# Conference Planning Guide: Technology Innovation Forum 2026

## Overview
This task has two main components: (1) curate pre-conference learning videos and (2) plan train travel for speakers.

## Task 1: Pre-Conference Video Curation
Fetch the top 6 AI-related videos from the Fireship YouTube channel (filter by titles containing AI/LLM keywords). These will be recommended to attendees for pre-conference preparation.

Selection criteria: Most-viewed videos with titles mentioning ai, gpt, llm, deepseek, neural, or similar AI topics.

## Task 2: Speaker Train Travel Planning
Plan travel for 3 speakers traveling to Qufu on 2026-03-11 (return 2026-03-14). Two speakers travel from Beijing, one from Shanghai.

## Excel Sheet Structure

### Sheet: Pre_Conference_Videos
Columns:
- Rank: 1-6 (1 = most viewed)
- Video_Title: Full YouTube video title
- View_Count: Total views
- Like_Count: Total likes
- Published_Date: Publication date
- URL: Video URL
- Relevance_Topic: AI sub-topic (e.g., LLM, GPT, Neural Networks)

### Sheet: Speaker_Travel
Columns:
- Speaker_Name: Full name
- From_City: Departure city
- Train_Number: Selected train
- Departure_Date: 2026-03-11 or 2026-03-14
- Departure_Time: HH:MM
- Arrival_Time: HH:MM
- Class: Travel class
- Price_CNY: Ticket price

## Notion Page Structure
Create a Notion page titled "Technology Innovation Forum 2026 - Planning" with sections:
1. Conference Overview (dates, venue, speaker list)
2. Pre-Conference Learning Resources (table of 6 videos)
3. Speaker Travel Summary (table from Speaker_Travel sheet)
4. Action Items checklist

## Calendar Event Format
- Create one travel event per speaker (outbound and return)
- Title: "[Speaker Name] - Travel to/from Qufu ([Train Number])"
- Invite the speaker using their email address from conference_config.json
