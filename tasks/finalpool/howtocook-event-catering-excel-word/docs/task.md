You are working for an event catering company that has been hired to plan a corporate team building event menu for 30 people. Your task is to design a complete catering plan and get client approval.

Use the HowToCook recipe database to browse recipes across different categories. Select at least 8 dishes to create a full catering menu with appetizers, main courses, and at least one dessert or soup. Choose recipes that would work well for a corporate team building setting serving 30 people.

Create an Excel workbook called Catering_Plan.xlsx in your workspace with two sheets. The first sheet must be named Menu and include the columns Dish_Name, Category (use values like Appetizer, Main_Course, Soup, or Dessert), Servings_For_30 (the number of servings adjusted for 30 people, usually 6 times the recipe default), Prep_Time_Minutes (estimated preparation time as a number), and Estimated_Cost_USD (estimated cost in US dollars as a number, estimate $3-8 per serving for most dishes). The Menu sheet should have at least 8 data rows. The second sheet must be named Ingredients and include the columns Dish_Name, Ingredient, Quantity_For_30_People, and Unit.

Create a Word document called Catering_Proposal.docx in your workspace. Include an executive summary describing the event and your catering approach, a menu overview section listing all selected dishes with brief descriptions, and a preparation timeline section describing when each category of dish will be prepared on the event day.

Create a Google Form called "Menu Approval Survey" with questions to collect client feedback on the proposed menu. Include at least three questions: one asking the client to rate overall menu satisfaction, one asking which dishes they want to confirm or remove, and one asking for any special dietary requirements or additional notes.

Send an email to client@corporate.com with subject "Catering Proposal for Team Building Event" that summarizes the proposed menu and mentions that the Excel plan, Word proposal, and feedback form have been prepared.
