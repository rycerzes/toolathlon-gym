# Financial Literacy Workshop Guidelines

## Performance Tier Definitions

Students are categorized based on their average score percentage across all graded submissions in a course:

- **Needs Support** (below 60%): Students who are struggling with fundamental concepts and need intensive, guided instruction.
- **Developing** (60% to 74.99%): Students who have a basic grasp but need reinforcement and practice with real-world applications.
- **Proficient** (75% and above): Students who demonstrate strong understanding and are ready for advanced topics and independent analysis.

## Workshop Format

Each workshop is 90 minutes long and should be scheduled at 2:00 PM on a weekday. Avoid scheduling conflicts with existing calendar events (check the 1 PM to 4 PM window).

## Workshop Topics

1. **Intro to Markets**: Targeted at "Needs Support" students. Covers basic market terminology, how to read stock quotes, and understanding daily price movements.
2. **Portfolio Basics**: Targeted at "Developing" students. Covers portfolio construction, diversification principles, and analyzing stock performance.
3. **Risk Management**: Targeted at "Proficient" students. Covers volatility analysis, risk metrics, and advanced portfolio strategies.

## Attendance Estimates

Use the total count of students in the target tier across both courses as the expected attendance for each workshop.
