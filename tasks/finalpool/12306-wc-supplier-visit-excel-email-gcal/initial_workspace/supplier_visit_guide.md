Supplier Visit Planning Guide

Supplier Prioritization
Suppliers should be ranked by stock criticality. A supplier is assigned High priority if the product has current stock below 5 units and is among the top sellers. Medium priority is assigned if current stock is between 5 and 15 units. Low priority is assigned if current stock exceeds 15 units. When two suppliers have the same priority level, the one with higher monthly sales rank takes precedence.

Travel Buffer Time
After the train arrives at the destination, allow a 30-minute buffer for travel from the station to the supplier's facility before scheduling the first meeting. For example, if the train to Shanghai arrives at 12:31, the earliest feasible meeting start time is 13:00.

Visit Schedule Format
Each row in the Visit_Schedule sheet should have a Date in YYYY-MM-DD format, the Supplier_Name exactly as it appears in the Products sheet, the City, the Meeting_Time in HH:MM format, a brief Purpose description (e.g., Stock replenishment negotiation), and Status set to Scheduled for all planned visits.

Priority Scale
High: current_stock less than 5 units
Medium: current_stock between 5 and 15 units inclusive
Low: current_stock greater than 15 units
