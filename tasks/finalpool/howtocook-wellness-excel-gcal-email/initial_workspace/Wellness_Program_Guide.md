Corporate Wellness Program Guide

Program Overview:
The Corporate Wellness Meal Plan is designed to support employee health and productivity
through mindful nutrition choices during the workweek.

Program Goals:
1. Reduce workplace stress through balanced nutrition
2. Improve mental focus and cognitive performance
3. Promote energy stability throughout the workday
4. Encourage healthy eating habits among all staff

Meal Plan Guidelines:
Breakfast: Choose energizing, protein-rich foods to start the day
Lunch: Focus on balanced meals with vegetables, protein, and complex carbohydrates
Dinner: Light, nutritious options that support recovery and rest

Nutritional Principles:
- Include a variety of colorful vegetables daily
- Maintain adequate protein intake for sustained energy
- Limit processed foods and excess sugar
- Stay hydrated throughout the workday
- Eat mindfully without distractions

Healthy Lunch Break:
The daily Healthy Lunch Break event encourages employees to step away from their desks,
enjoy a proper meal, and recharge for the afternoon. This 1-hour break is scheduled at
noon each weekday.

Expected Outcomes:
- 20% reduction in afternoon energy slumps
- Improved team morale and collaboration
- Better focus during afternoon meetings
- Reduced sick days due to improved immune function
