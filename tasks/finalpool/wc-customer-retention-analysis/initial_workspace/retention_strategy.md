Customer Retention Playbook
===========================

Last updated: February 2026

Customer Segmentation Rules:
- VIP: Total lifetime spending exceeds $500
- Regular: Total lifetime spending between $100 and $500
- New: Total lifetime spending under $100

Churn Risk Assessment:
Customers are considered "at risk" if they have not placed an order
within the churn threshold period (typically 90 days based on industry
benchmarks). The exact threshold should be confirmed against the
current industry benchmarks API.

Retention Priority:
1. VIP at-risk customers get the highest priority
2. Regular at-risk customers are contacted in the next cycle
3. New at-risk customers receive automated campaigns

Email Outreach Guidelines:
- Use personalized subject lines with the customer's first name
- Reference their inactivity period
- Send from retention@store.com
- Log all outreach in the tracking spreadsheet
