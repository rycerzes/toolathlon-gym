Media Analysis Project - Channel Comparison

Purpose: Compare the performance and content strategy of two YouTube channels in the video database.

Channels to compare:
- Fireship (technology channel, short-form content)
- Veritasium (science channel, long-form documentaries)

Required outputs:
1. Cloud spreadsheet titled "Channel Comparison Analysis" with:
   - Comparison sheet: one row per channel with all metrics
   - Monthly_Fireship sheet: monthly video counts for Fireship

2. Word document: Channel_Analysis_Report.docx with sections:
   - Executive Summary
   - Channel Metrics Comparison
   - Key Findings
   - Recommendations

3. Email to media@company.com with subject "YouTube Channel Comparison Report"

Metrics to compute per channel:
- Video_Count, Total_Views, Total_Likes
- Avg_Views (rounded to 0 decimals)
- Avg_Duration_Sec (rounded to 0 decimals)
- Avg_Like_Rate (like_count/view_count*100, rounded to 2 decimals)
- Top_Video_Title, Top_Video_Views
