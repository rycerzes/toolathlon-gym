# Project Portfolio Management Guide

Project Selection Criteria:
- Strategic alignment with department goals
- Available team capacity
- Expected ROI
- Risk assessment

Team Assignment Rules:
- Project lead: Top performer in relevant department
- Team size: 3-7 members per project
- Cross-functional teams preferred
- Max 3 concurrent projects per person

Timeline Guidelines:
- Standard project duration: 3-6 months
- Kickoff within 2 weeks of approval
- Monthly progress reviews
- Quarterly portfolio review
