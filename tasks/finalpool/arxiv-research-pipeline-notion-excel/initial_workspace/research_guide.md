# Research Synthesis Guide

Paper Selection Criteria:
- Published in top venues (NeurIPS, ICML, ACL, EMNLP)
- High citation count or recent publication
- Novel methodology or significant results
- Relevant to team's research direction

Analysis Dimensions:
- Methodology innovation
- Experimental setup and benchmarks
- Reproducibility potential
- Application to our use cases

Knowledge Base Organization:
- Categorize by research topic
- Link related papers
- Extract actionable insights
- Identify open problems
