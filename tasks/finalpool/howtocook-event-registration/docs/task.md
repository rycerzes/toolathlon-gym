The HR department is organizing a Lunch and Learn Chinese Cooking event for employees. You need to set up the entire event infrastructure.

First, browse the recipe database and select 2 dishes that are relatively simple from different categories. Note the dish names, their ingredients, and their preparation steps.

Second, create a registration form titled "Lunch and Learn: Chinese Cooking Registration" with five questions. The first question should be "Full Name" as a required short text field. The second should be "Email" as a required short text field. The third should be "Department" as a required single choice field with options Engineering, Marketing, Finance, HR, and Operations. The fourth should be "Dietary Restrictions" as an optional checkbox field with options Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-free, Nut-free, and None. The fifth should be "Which dish are you most excited about?" as a required single choice field listing the 2 selected dish names as options.

Third, create a calendar event titled "Lunch and Learn: Chinese Cooking" scheduled for March 20, 2026 from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM. Set the location to "Company Kitchen". In the event description, include the names of the two selected dishes.

Fourth, create a page titled "Cooking Event Planning" that includes the selected dishes with their ingredients and preparation steps, along with event logistics including the date March 20, 2026, time 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM, and location Company Kitchen.

Fifth, send an announcement email from hr@company.com to all-staff@company.com with the subject "You're Invited: Lunch and Learn Chinese Cooking - March 20" that describes the event, mentions both selected dishes, and includes the date, time, and location.
