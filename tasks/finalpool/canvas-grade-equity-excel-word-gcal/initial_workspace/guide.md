# Grade Equity Analysis Guide

## Matching Fall Courses Across Years

Fall semester courses are identified by the "J" suffix in their course codes (e.g., AAA-2013J for Fall 2013). To compare year-over-year, match courses that share the same base course code prefix but differ in the year portion. For example, AAA-2013J and AAA-2014J are the same course offered in consecutive fall semesters.

## Computing Grade Distributions

For each course, gather all student submission scores across all assignments. Average each student's scores to get a single per-student mean. Then compute course-level statistics from those per-student means.

Grade bands:
- A: 90-100
- B: 70-89
- C: 50-69
- D: 30-49
- F: 0-29

## Equity Metrics

Compare the mean score for Fall 2013 vs Fall 2014 for each matched course pair.

- Score Difference = Fall 2014 Mean - Fall 2013 Mean
- Equity Status based on |Score Difference|:
  - Less than 5 points: Acceptable
  - 5 to 10 points: Concerning
  - Greater than 10 points: Action Required

## Pass Rate

Pass rate = percentage of students with a mean score >= 50.
