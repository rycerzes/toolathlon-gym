# Dynamic Pricing Model

Price Adjustment Factors:
- Commodity price trends (gold, energy)
- Market index movements
- Product demand elasticity
- Inventory levels
- Competitive positioning

Adjustment Rules:
- High elasticity products: Max 5% change per quarter
- Low elasticity products: Max 10% change per quarter
- Never price below cost (minimum 10% margin)
- Premium products: Follow market trends
- Budget products: Counter-cyclical pricing

Impact Estimation:
Revenue Impact = Sum(Current Revenue * Price Change %)
