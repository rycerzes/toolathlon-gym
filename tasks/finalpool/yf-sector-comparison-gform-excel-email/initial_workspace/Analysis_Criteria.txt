Sector Comparison Analysis Criteria
====================================

The following financial metrics should be gathered and compared for each stock:

1. Market Capitalization (in billions USD, rounded to 2 decimal places)
2. Current Stock Price (USD)
3. Trailing Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio
4. 52-Week High Price
5. 52-Week Low Price

For the Sector_Summary sheet, classify each stock's current price as:
- Above_Avg: if the stock's price is above the average price of all five stocks
- Below_Avg: if the stock's price is at or below the average price of all five stocks

Average price reference: (GOOGL + AMZN + JPM + JNJ + XOM prices) / 5
