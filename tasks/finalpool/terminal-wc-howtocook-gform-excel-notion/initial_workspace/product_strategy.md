# Meal Kit Product Line Strategy

## Vision
Launch a curated meal kit product line that bundles our existing kitchen appliances with recipe cards, pre-portioned ingredient guides, and cooking tips. Each kit targets a specific cooking style and skill level.

## Target Segments
- Home cooking beginners who want guided experiences
- Busy professionals looking for quick meal solutions
- Food enthusiasts exploring new cuisines
- Gift buyers seeking unique kitchen bundles

## Key Goals
1. Increase average order value by 40% through bundling
2. Reduce product return rate by pairing appliances with clear use cases
3. Build recurring revenue through recipe card subscription add-ons
4. Launch 5 initial kits within Q2 2026

## Product Line Principles
- Each kit must include exactly one appliance and at least 3 recipe cards
- Recipes should match the appliance's primary cooking method
- Kits priced at appliance cost + $15 for recipe cards and packaging
- Priority given to kits with the most recipe variety

## Success Metrics
- Customer survey response rate > 20%
- At least 3 "High Priority" kits identified
- Recipe compatibility coverage > 60% of catalog
