Then search for recent scholarly papers related to topics like "machine learning", "data analytics", and "computational thinking" to understand current research directions in our teaching areas.

Create an Excel file called Curriculum_Review_Report.xlsx with three sheets. The third sheet Gap_Analysis should have Metric and Value columns with Total_Courses, Papers_Reviewed, High_Relevance_Papers, Curriculum_Coverage_Pct (round to 1 decimal), and Top_Gap_Area.

Create a Notion page titled "Curriculum Innovation Tracker" with a heading "Research-Curriculum Alignment Review" and paragraphs summarizing the review findings, listing top 3 research trends not adequately covered in current courses, and recommending new course topics.
