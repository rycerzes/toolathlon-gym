# Curriculum Review Standards

Review Criteria:
- Course content alignment with industry trends
- Research topic coverage in course materials
- Enrollment demand vs course offerings
- Assessment quality and rigor

Relevance Scoring:
- High: Paper topic directly maps to course content
- Medium: Paper topic is adjacent to course content
- Low: Paper topic has tangential relevance

Gap Identification:
- Topics with high research activity but no course coverage
- Courses with declining enrollment vs emerging alternatives
