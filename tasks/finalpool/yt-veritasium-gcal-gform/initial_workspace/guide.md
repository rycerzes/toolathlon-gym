# Task Guide: Veritasium Science Enrichment Program

## Overview
Plan a 5-session science enrichment program using top Veritasium 2025 videos.

## Step 1: Find Videos
Query the video database for Veritasium videos published in 2025 (Jan 1 - Dec 31, 2025).
Select the top 5 by view count.

## Deliverable 1: GCal Events (5 sessions)
Dates (consecutive Wednesdays): 2026-04-01, 2026-04-08, 2026-04-15, 2026-04-22, 2026-04-29
Time: 14:00 - 15:30
Title format: "Science Enrichment: [video title truncated to 50 chars]"
Description: must include view count and duration in seconds

## Deliverable 2: Google Form
Title: "Science Enrichment Program Feedback"
Question 1 (RADIO): "Which video topic interested you most?" - options = all 5 full video titles
Question 2 (RADIO): "How would you rate the difficulty level?" - options: Too Easy, Just Right, Too Hard
Question 3 (RADIO): "How often should we hold these sessions?" - options: Weekly, Bi-weekly, Monthly
Question 4 (TEXT): "What other science topics would you like to explore?"

## Deliverable 3: Viewing_Schedule.xlsx
Sheet name: Sessions
Columns: Session_Num (1-5), Date (YYYY-MM-DD), Video_ID, Title, View_Count, Duration_Min (1 decimal)
Sort: Session_Num ASC

## Deliverable 4: Email
To: students@school.edu
Subject: "Science Enrichment Sessions Scheduled"
Body: List all 5 dates and corresponding video titles
