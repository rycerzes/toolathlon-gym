Financial Health Benchmarks

Profit Margin is defined as Net Income divided by Total Revenue, expressed as a percentage.

Profit Margin greater than 15%: Strong
Profit Margin between 5% and 15% (inclusive of 5%): Adequate
Profit Margin less than 5%: Weak

Use these benchmarks to classify each company's margin health in your financial health assessment.
