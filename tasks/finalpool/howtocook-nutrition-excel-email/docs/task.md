Our nutrition team is putting together a breakfast recipe report. Please look up breakfast recipes from the recipe database and compile the information into a spreadsheet and email.

Create an Excel file called "Breakfast_Nutrition_Report.xlsx" in my workspace with two sheets.

The first sheet, "Recipes", should list each breakfast recipe with columns: Name, Category, Ingredients_Count (the number of distinct ingredients), and Difficulty (easy/medium/hard based on the number of ingredients: 5 or fewer is easy, 6 to 10 is medium, more than 10 is hard).

The second sheet, "Summary", should be a table with columns Metric and Value containing: Total_Recipes (the count of breakfast recipes found), Avg_Ingredients (the average number of ingredients across all recipes, rounded to one decimal place), and Easiest_Recipe (the recipe with the fewest ingredients).

After creating the spreadsheet, send an email from nutrition-team@company.com to dietician@company.com with the subject "Breakfast Recipe Nutrition Report". The email body should summarize how many breakfast recipes were found and provide a brief overview of the findings.
