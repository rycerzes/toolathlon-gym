# Academic Intervention Framework

Risk Classification:
- Critical (score < 50): Immediate mandatory tutoring
- At Risk (score 50-65): Weekly check-ins with advisor
- On Track (score > 65): Standard monitoring

Intervention Priorities:
1. Courses with risk rate > 50% - department-level response
2. Individual critical students - personal outreach
3. At-risk trend monitoring - weekly reports

Escalation Protocol:
- Critical students: Notify within 24 hours
- Department risk rate > 40%: Dean notification
