Academic Accreditation Standards

Effective: Academic Year 2025-2026 | Published by the University Accreditation Standards Board

Course Component Requirements

All courses offered by the university must meet the following minimum requirements to maintain accreditation compliance:

Criterion Minimum Requirement Description
Assignments 8 per course Each course must include at least 8 graded assignments to ensure adequate assessment of student learning outcomes.
Quizzes 3 per course Each course must include at least 3 quizzes to provide regular formative assessment and feedback opportunities.

Compliance Classification

Status Definition
CompliantCourse meets ALL minimum requirements listed above
Non-CompliantCourse fails to meet ONE OR MORE of the minimum requirements
Important: Non-compliant courses must submit a remediation plan to the department head by April 15, 2026. Departments with non-compliant courses will receive notification via the accreditation review process. Each department is identified by the subject area prefix in the course name (e.g., "Applied Analytics & Algorithms" courses belong to the Applied Analytics department, "Biochemistry & Bioinformatics" courses belong to the Biochemistry department).

Review Process

The curriculum review committee will:

  1. Query the learning management system for all current course data
  2. Evaluate each course against the standards above
  3. Generate a compliance report documenting findings
  4. Notify department heads of any non-compliant courses
  5. Track remediation progress through the follow-up system
