# Internal Role to Market Benchmark Mapping

This document describes how internal job titles map to standard market benchmark categories.

## Engineering Department
- DevOps Engineer -> Market Category: DevOps Engineer
- ML Engineer -> Market Category: ML Engineer
- Software Engineer -> Market Category: Software Engineer

## Finance Department
- Analyst -> Market Category: Analyst
- Senior Analyst -> Market Category: Senior Analyst

## HR Department
- HR Executive -> Market Category: HR Executive
- HR Manager -> Market Category: HR Manager

## Operations Department
- Operations Coordinator -> Market Category: Operations Coordinator
- Ops Manager -> Market Category: Ops Manager

## R&D Department
- Researcher -> Market Category: Researcher
- Scientist -> Market Category: Scientist

## Sales Department
- Account Executive -> Market Category: Account Executive
- Sales Manager -> Market Category: Sales Manager

## Support Department
- Customer Specialist -> Market Category: Customer Specialist
- Support Engineer -> Market Category: Support Engineer

## Notes
- Market benchmarks represent the 50th percentile (median) of industry compensation
- Benchmarks are updated annually from industry salary surveys
- Pay ratio is calculated as: Internal Average Salary / Market Benchmark
