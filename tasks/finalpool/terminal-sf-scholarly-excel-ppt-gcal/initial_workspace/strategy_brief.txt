Q1 2026 Sales Strategy Brief
============================

Strategic Goals:
1. Increase overall revenue by 12% compared to Q4 2025
2. Improve performance in underperforming regions through targeted initiatives
3. Expand Enterprise and Government segment penetration
4. Reduce reliance on top-performing products by diversifying the portfolio

Revenue Targets by Region:
- Europe: $170,000
- Asia Pacific: $165,000
- North America: $160,000
- Middle East: $155,000
- Latin America: $145,000

Key Priorities:
- Regions performing below the median must receive Priority Focus designation
- All action items must be backed by data and, where possible, academic research
- The VP presentation to the board is scheduled for mid-March 2026

Date: February 28, 2026
