# Market-Sales Correlation Analysis

Methodology:
- Collect stock price data for key market indicators
- Gather internal sales data by month
- Calculate Pearson correlation coefficients
- Identify leading/lagging indicators

Interpretation Guide:
- Correlation > 0.7: Strong positive relationship
- Correlation 0.3-0.7: Moderate relationship
- Correlation < 0.3: Weak relationship
- Negative correlation: Inverse relationship
