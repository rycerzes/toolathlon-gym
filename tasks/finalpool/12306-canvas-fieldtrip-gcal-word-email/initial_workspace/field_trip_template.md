This template guides the creation of official university field trip notices.

Required Sections:

Section 1 - Trip Overview:
Summarize the purpose of the trip and its academic significance.
State the travel dates clearly.
Include the total per-student cost prominently.
Mention the destination and key sites to be visited.

Section 2 - Purpose and Academic Significance:
Explain how this trip connects to the course curriculum.
Describe the educational value of the specific sites.
Note any academic activities planned at the destination.

Section 3 - Travel Details and Schedule:
List the outbound train details: train number, departure station, departure time, arrival station, arrival time.
List the return train details with the same information.
Include the travel duration for each leg.

Section 4 - Student Requirements:
Students must bring a valid student identification card.
Students must follow all university safety guidelines.
Students must attend all scheduled visits and activities.
Students should dress appropriately for cultural heritage sites.

Section 5 - Cost Breakdown:
Outbound ticket price (second-class seat)
Return ticket price (second-class seat)
Total round-trip cost per student

Cost calculation: ticket price times 2 (round trip) equals total cost per student.
The second-class ticket price for Beijing South to Qufu East is 553 CNY each way.
Therefore total round trip cost is 553 times 2 equals 1106 CNY per student.

Section 6 - Cancellation Policy:
The trip may be cancelled due to extreme weather or safety concerns.
Students who cannot attend should notify the instructor at least 48 hours in advance.
