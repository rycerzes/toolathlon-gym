Curriculum Review Report Template

The final report should follow this general structure:

Title: Curriculum-Research Alignment Review

Executive Summary: A brief overview of findings, including the number of courses reviewed, assignments analyzed, papers identified, and the overall alignment assessment.

Course Overview: Description of each course section, listing all assignments with their point values and due dates.

Literature Review: Summary of the relevant academic papers found, including their key contributions and relevance to the curriculum.

Alignment Analysis: Detailed results of the assignment-to-paper alignment scoring, highlighting strong and weak alignments.

Recommendations: Specific suggestions for improving curriculum alignment with current research, including potential new topics or updated assignment designs.

The report should reference specific data points from the analysis and provide actionable insights for the curriculum committee.
