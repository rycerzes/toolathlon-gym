Team Learning Channel Tracking - Q1 2026 Initiative

The channel covers topics including web development, AI, cloud services, and programming languages.

- Identify which months have the highest viewer engagement

Output requirements:
- Monthly statistics spreadsheet with view and like data
- Team knowledge base summary page
- Email notification to analytics team upon completion
