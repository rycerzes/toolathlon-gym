Product Category Notes - Q1 2026
=================================

TV & Home Theater:
Strong performance driven by 4K and 8K display demand. Premium models
are selling well. Supply chain has stabilized.

Electronics:
Broadest category with consistent demand. Mix of accessories and
mid-range devices. Price competition is increasing.

Audio:
Wireless and smart speaker segments driving growth. Headphone sales
remain strong. Premium audio products gaining traction.

Cameras:
Niche market with enthusiast buyers. Smartphone competition continues
to pressure point-and-shoot segment. Mirrorless cameras showing
promise.

Watches:
Smartwatch integration boosting the segment. Traditional watches
maintain a collector market. Average prices holding steady.

Home Appliances:
Market saturation affecting growth. Focus on energy-efficient models.
Need to evaluate product line for underperformers.
