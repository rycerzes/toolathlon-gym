Key Insights

Growth Opportunities

Audio products represent the highest growth opportunity with consistent double-digit growth projected throughout 2026. TV and Home Theater shows strong seasonal potential particularly in Q4 (holiday season). Watches segment is accelerating due to smartwatch innovation.

Areas of Concern

Home Appliances may recover in the second half of the year but remain the weakest category. Camera growth remains below market average. Electronics growth is stable but competitive pressure may limit margin expansion.

Recommendations

Focus inventory investment on Audio and TV & Home Theater categories. Consider promotional campaigns for Home Appliances to stimulate demand. Evaluate Camera product line for potential SKU rationalization.
