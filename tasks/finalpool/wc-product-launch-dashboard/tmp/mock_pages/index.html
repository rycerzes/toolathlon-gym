Consumer Trends Summary

The audio segment continues to outperform expectations driven by wireless and smart speaker adoption. TV and home theater is seeing renewed interest due to large-format display price declines. Home appliances face headwinds from market saturation and economic uncertainty. Electronics remains the largest category by market size with steady growth. Camera sales are being impacted by smartphone improvements but maintain a niche enthusiast market. Watches are benefiting from the smartwatch segment.
