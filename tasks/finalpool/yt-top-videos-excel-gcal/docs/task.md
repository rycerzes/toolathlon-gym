You are a team lead who wants to set up a structured learning program using the most popular technology videos from the Fireship channel. Your task is to curate the top videos and schedule dedicated team viewing sessions in the shared calendar.

Start by querying the video database to find the top 10 most-viewed videos published by the Fireship channel. Rank them from 1 (highest view count) to 10 (lowest).

Create a spreadsheet file called Top_Videos_Watchlist.xlsx in your workspace. This file must contain two sheets.

The first sheet must be named Watchlist. It should have one row per video with the following columns in this exact order: Rank (1 through 10), Video_ID, Title, Duration_Min (the video duration converted to minutes, rounded to 1 decimal place), View_Count, Like_Count, and Like_Rate (calculated as like_count divided by view_count multiplied by 100, rounded to 2 decimal places). Sort rows by View_Count in descending order so Rank 1 is the most-viewed video.

The second sheet must be named Summary. It should have exactly two columns named Label and Value, with the following rows in order: Total_Selected (which should be 10), Combined_Views (the sum of all 10 videos' view counts), Avg_Duration_Min (average duration in minutes rounded to 1 decimal), and Avg_Like_Rate (average like rate across all 10 videos rounded to 2 decimal places).

After creating the spreadsheet, schedule one learning session in the shared calendar for each of the 10 videos. Each event title should be "Team Learning: " followed by the video title truncated to 40 characters if it is longer than 40 characters. Schedule the sessions on consecutive Tuesdays starting April 7, 2026. The first session goes on April 7, the second on April 14, the third on April 21, continuing forward for all 10 sessions. Each session runs from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM.

Finally, send an email to team@company.com with subject "Learning Sessions Scheduled" and a message body listing all 10 sessions with their scheduled dates and video titles.
